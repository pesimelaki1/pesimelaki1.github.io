About

Jana is a Tongan AP student at Aragon High School and is currently working to start a non-profit organization geared towards the advancement of Polynesians in computer science. She is also the Outreach Director of Digitize Hackathon. She had the opportunity to study at Facebook Headquarters with Girls Who Code and specializes in Java, Python, and HTML/CSS. Additionally, she is passionate about social justice, taking the initiative to become a March For Our Lives San Mateo Branch Organizer and lobbying state government officials for common sense gun reform. She is grateful to have earned the San Mateo Youth Foundation Scholarship at 14 and been apart of the National Society of High School Scholars for 3 years. She also has been a motivated Vice President for 1 and President for 2 years for her class. In her spare time, she enjoys the art of daring fashion and special effects makeup. She has also been dedicated lacrosse, basketball, and acting in local theater.

Final Project

The original fall project was the class "Saw", which simulated the plot of the movie. This semester, I have added the parent class, Movie. Saw implements the Entertainment interface. I added a simple Board Game class and Instrument class to show what else can implement the Entertainment interface. To tie them all together, I put them in a driver class, Store, made to simulate one, where movies, instruments, and board games can all be bought.
